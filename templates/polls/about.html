About Blood Donation

Join our mission to save lives through safe and voluntary blood donation. Every drop counts, and your contribution can make a difference.

Become a Donor

Benefits of Donating Blood

Emotional Benefits

Experience the joy of saving lives and making a positive impact in your community.

Health Benefits

Regular donation helps maintain healthy iron levels and reduces the risk of heart disease.

Free Health Checkup

Every blood donation includes a comprehensive health screening at no cost to you.

Blood Tests

Complete blood count and iron levels

Vital Signs

Blood pressure, pulse, and temperature

Health History

Comprehensive health questionnaire

Infection Screening

Testing for various blood-borne diseases

The Donation Process

Simple, safe, and takes only about an hour of your time

1
Registration

Complete a donor registration form and provide identification

2
Screening

Quick health check and medical history review

3
Donation

Actual donation takes about 8-10 minutes

4
Recovery

Rest and refreshments for 10-15 minutes

Basic Requirements

Age

Between 18 and 65 years old

Weight

Minimum 50 kg (110 lbs)

Health

Good general health and feeling well

Frequency

Wait at least 56 days between donations

Before You Donate

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water before and after donation

Eat Well

Have a healthy meal 3 hours before donation

Get Rest

Ensure you're well-rested before donation
